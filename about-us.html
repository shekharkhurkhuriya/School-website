**About Our School**

HPS Convent School, Nagaur was established in the year 2018 with a vision to provide quality education in an English medium environment. The school is committed to shaping young minds with knowledge, discipline, and strong moral values.

Under the guidance of our respected Director, Mr. R.K. Danga, the school has been continuously growing and maintaining high standards of education. His leadership and dedication have played a vital role in building a strong academic foundation for students.

The foundation and inspiration of this institution come from Mr. Manak Ram Choudhary, who is successfully running Hind Public Senior Secondary School. His contribution to the field of education continues to inspire us to deliver excellence and create a better future for our students.

Our school offers classes from Nursery to 10th and focuses on overall development including academics, discipline, and co-curricular activities. With a team of 22 dedicated staff members, we ensure a supportive and engaging learning environment for every student.

At HPS Convent School, we believe in nurturing talent, encouraging creativity, and preparing students to face future challenges with confidence.
